About Me

Welcome to my portfolio!

My name is Allison Crow, and I am an aspiring artist who loves any oppurtunity to use her creative skills for good. I got my Bachelor's degree in Psychology and spent a few years working with children who have special needs.
However, I recently ended up working in a warehouse doing data entry and inventory work, and falling in love with the different systems and processes involved with what I get to do on a daily basis.
2021 has been one of the hardest years of my life; for different reasons than most people. It has been filled with lots of challenges, and at several points I felt as though I was starting over entirely. When presented with the oppurtunity to return to school and study Web Development, I thought it was a good time to to start a new chapter in my life.
Please feel free to browse my resume and web design projects. Also below, you'll find my art and photography, as well. As an artist, a lot of thought, detail, and heart go into everything I create.
